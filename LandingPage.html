Why was TakeStock created?

TakeStock was born out of a personal need for a comprehensive stock analysis system. Like many investors and traders, I spent significant time collecting and processing data from various sources and analyzing using tools such as Excel and Jupyter Notebook.

Existing systems in the market often lack either depth or usability. Those with rich data sources and integrity may sacrifice usability and data visuals, while still requiring considerable time for data processing to perform sophisticated analysis. Additionally, none of them offer the capability to analyze multiple stocks across analytics with rich visuals and comprehensive information.

These challenges inspired the creation of TakeStock, an accessible and complete tool for researching and analyzing stocks, empowering users to make informed investment decisions and generate alpha returns.
